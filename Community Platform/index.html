About This Site



This is the official community page of Village of Bellmare!

This is a platform for the residents of Bellmare to enjoy and socialize with each other, creating a sense of communal unity as well as helping one another get through this tough time together!

Use the menu button to the right to navigate through the website, where you can find town updates, our own town social media page, as well as a chat page for people in our town to talk to one another!

Struggling? Get Help!



Nobody should be embarrassed or scared to ask for help, especially not during these tumultuous times.

Our community a loving haven where reaching out and asking for help isn't a sign of weakness, but a sign of courage and strength. Feel free to view our resources and use them accordingly, completely anonymously.

Below are links to mental health pages, emergency services, a chat room to talk to someone if you feel a little down, and a COVID-19 tracking page.

Remember, we're all in this fight against COVID-19 together; one resident's battle is the entire community's battle.
